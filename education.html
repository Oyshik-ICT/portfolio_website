Cantonment Public School and College,Rangpur, Bangladesh

Education Duration : Class 1 to 12 (Year 2005 - Year 2017)

This institution places a strong emphasis on discipline, and my 12 years here taught me valuable lessons in leading a disciplined life. One of the key takeaways was learning effective time management. The school instilled a strict adherence to schedules, teaching me the importance of punctuality. I concluded my time at this institution in 2017, and the experience left me with cherished memories. These formative years at Cantonment Public School and College have played a pivotal role in shaping who I am today.
