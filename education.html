Discover My Learning Journey: The Story of My Education

Islamic University, Kushtia, Bangladesh

Degree : B.Sc(Engg.) in Information and Communication Technology
Graduation Year : 2024

In the ICT department, I gained valuable insights into technology. While initially unfamiliar with programming and coding competitions, I learned these skills from our seniors. The curriculum covered a range of information and communication technology courses. Beyond academics, being part of a diverse public university exposed me to various backgrounds and perspectives. I developed the ability to interact and collaborate with people of different characteristics. This experience has significantly enhanced my communication skills, allowing me to engage effortlessly with a variety of individuals, a skill I didn't possess before joining the university.
